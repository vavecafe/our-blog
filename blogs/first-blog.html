Automate Your Dropbox File Management with n8n

Want to automate your file management within Dropbox? This tutorial will guide you through connecting n8n to your Dropbox account, enabling seamless automation of your file workflows.

Step 1: Create a Dropbox App

Navigate to Dropbox.com/developers/apps and click "Create App".

Step 2: Choose Scoped Access

Select "Scoped Access" and choose the access level: either access to your entire Dropbox account or a specific folder. This example uses full account access.

Step 3: Name Your App

Give your app a descriptive name (e.g., "n8n Integration").

Step 4: Create the App and Generate Access Token

Click "Create App". Once created, scroll down to "Generated Access Token" and click "Generate". Copy this key; you'll need it in the next step.

Step 5: Add a New Node in n8n

In your n8n workflow, add a new node. Search for the "Dropbox" node.

Step 6: Create n8n Credentials

Create new credentials, select "Access Token", paste the key you copied from Dropbox, and click "Save".

Step 7: Test the Connection

Test the connection to ensure n8n is successfully connected to your Dropbox account.

Step 8: Connection Successful!

You've successfully connected Dropbox to n8n!
